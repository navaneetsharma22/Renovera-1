# Renovera Design System

## Philosophy
Minimal, Bold, Architectural, Structured, Modern, Timeless, Professional, High-end.

## Colors
- **Primary:** `#18181B`
- **Secondary:** `#3F3F46`
- **Accent:** `#71717A`
- **Background:** `#FAFAFA`
- **Surface:** `#FFFFFF`
- **Heading:** `#09090B`
- **Body:** `#52525B`
- **States:** Success (`#16A34A`), Warning (`#D97706`), Error (`#DC2626`)
- **Border:** `#E5E7EB`
- **Divider:** `#F1F5F9`

*(DO NOT USE: Gold, Bronze, Terracotta, Green theme, Glassmorphism, Neon colors, Bright gradients)*

## Typography
- **Heading Font:** Geist (`--font-heading`)
- **Body Font:** Inter (`--font-sans`)

## Grid & Layout
- **Grid:** 12-column
- **Desktop Container:** 1440px
- **Content Width:** 1280px
- **Section Width:** 1200px
- **Mobile First Responsive Layout**

## Border Radius
Architectural corners (Sharp/Minimal). No pill-shaped elements.
- **Small:** 2px
- **Medium:** 4px
- **Large:** 8px

## Shadows
Extremely subtle shadows. Prefer borders over elevation. No floating material design look.

## Icons
Lucide Icons: Thin stroke, minimal, monochrome, consistent sizing, architectural appearance.

## Motion Principles
- **Use:** Fade, Slide, Scale, Reveal, Stagger, Image Mask Reveal, Section Entrance, Counter, Progress.
- **Avoid:** Bounce, Elastic, Rotate, Flip, Flash, Excessive parallax.
- **Hover Effects:** Subtle and professional.

## Components
- **Buttons:** Solid, minimal, no gradients, no glow, architectural.
- **Cards:** Minimal borders, generous spacing, clean hierarchy, architectural alignment.
